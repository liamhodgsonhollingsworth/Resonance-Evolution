## What is vendored

This is a **kit of individual pieces**, not a pre-assembled scene. The explorer assembles a small
walled dungeon room from the wall/floor/column pieces and scatters the props as grab-into-inventory
collectibles. Files under `glb/`:

- Structure (walls to run into): `wall.glb`, `wall-half.glb`, `wall-narrow.glb`, `wall-opening.glb`,
  `column.glb`, `gate.glb`, `stairs.glb`, `floor.glb`, `floor-detail.glb`, `dirt.glb`,
  `wood-structure.glb`, `wood-support.glb`.
- Props (collectibles): `barrel.glb`, `chest.glb`, `coin.glb`, `banner.glb`, `rocks.glb`,
  `stones.glb`, `trap.glb`, `shield-round.glb`, `shield-rectangle.glb`, `weapon-sword.glb`,
  `weapon-spear.glb`.
- Characters (not used by the explorer, kept for completeness): `character-human.glb`,
  `character-orc.glb`.
